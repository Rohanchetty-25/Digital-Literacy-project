# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist for College Students

## DO's ✅

1. **Do maintain a professional profile picture** - Use a clear, appropriate photo that presents you well to potential employers and recruiters.

2. **Do customize your privacy settings** - Regularly review and update privacy controls to limit who can see your personal information and posts.

3. **Do think before you post** - Ask yourself: "Would I be comfortable if my professor, parent, or future employer saw this?"

4. **Do showcase your achievements** - Share projects, certifications, and academic accomplishments to build a positive digital footprint.

5. **Do use strong, unique passwords** - Enable two-factor authentication on all social media accounts for added security.

---

## DON'Ts ❌

1. **Don't share personal information publicly** - Avoid posting your phone number, home address, or real-time location updates.

2. **Don't engage in online arguments** - Avoid heated debates or inflammatory comments that could damage your reputation.

3. **Don't post during class or late at night** - Your posting habits reflect your priorities and time management.

4. **Don't accept friend requests from strangers** - Be selective about who you connect with to avoid scams and unwanted attention.

5. **Don't post about confidential matters** - Avoid sharing details about exams, assignments, or others' personal information without permission.

---

*Remember: Your digital footprint is permanent. What you post today can impact your career opportunities tomorrow.*
